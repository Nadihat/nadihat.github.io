Intention Repeater MAX

A Radionics tool to Repeat your Intentions

   






A Digital Buddhist Prayer Wheel

The Intention Repeater is a Radionics tool that empowers users to manifest their desires by repeating their intentions at an astonishing rate of over a million million times per second. As an open-source application, it offers an efficient and effective way to focus your thoughts and energy on your goals. By inputting your intention directly into the interface, the Intention Repeater amplifies your intention and potentially brings you closer to your desired outcome. Harness the power of repetition and focus your intentions with this lightweight, no-frills approach to intention manifestation.
